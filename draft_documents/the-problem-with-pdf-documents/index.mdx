## PDFs are great, aren't they?

For people or businesses wishing to pass information, PDFs are great. There are designers capable of producing fantastic, eye catching documents which promote goods as well as websites, without the requirement of being online. The core benefit of a PDF document is that once saved, the data, images, design etc. are all preserved exactly as intended.
